About

Full Name: Kim-Rio Pancras
Nationality: Brazilian (with Japanese heritage)
Genre: Contemporary Fiction / Magical Realism
Notable Works: The Whispering Leaves (2023), Beneath the Neon Sky (2021)

Background:
Born in São Paulo, Kim-Rio Pancras blends urban grit with poetic mysticism,
drawing from Brazil’s vibrant culture and immigrant experiences. A former street artist,
their writing is visually rich and emotionally raw.

Style & Themes:
Lyrical prose with bursts of surrealism.
Explores identity, displacement, and hidden magic in everyday life.
Inspired by Haruki Murakami, Clarice Lispector, and Jorge Amado.

Trivia:
Writes best at 3 AM with jazz playing.
Never revises-edits only after the first draft is complete.
Their next novel, The Paper Foxes, is due in 2025.
